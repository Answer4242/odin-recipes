Macaroni & Cheese

Wonderful and easy home-cooked Macaroni & Cheese

This is an excellent recipe that rivals some of the best fine dining restaurant Mac & Cheese you could order, but at home.

Ingredients

  • 8oz. elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 - 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Black pepper to taste
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese (Sharp)

Steps

  1. Boil the macaroni until al dente - about 7 minutes.
  2. Make a roux by heating the butter in a sucepan and combining the flour. Stir in the milk and stir until creamy.
  3. Add a cheese layer and then another layer of noodles
  4. Add more sauce and layer of noodles
  5. Cover with foil and bake for 45 minutes in a pre-heated 450 degree oven
