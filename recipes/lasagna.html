Lasagna

Wonderful and easy home-cooked Lasagna

My mom has a unique take on lasagna that features a rich, homemade meat sauce and a wonderfully cheesy filling. Instead of the usual ricotta, she uses a mix of small-curd cottage cheese and Parmesan, which makes it stand out. I have never tasted anything quite like it! Pair it with a fresh green salad and some crusty garlic bread for the perfect meal.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb. ground beef
  • 1/2 lb. ground pork
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • Canned, crushed tomatoes
  • Sugar
  • Lasagna noodles (no-boil)
  • Cheese: cottage, parmesan, and shredded mozzarella
  • Eggs

    • Steps

    1. Combine and cook the meat until browned. Add the onion, crushed tomatoes, and sugar. Cook together until the onion is translucent
    2. Put a layer of sauce on the bottom of the baking dish and add a layer of noodles
    3. Add a cheese layer and then another layer of noodles
    4. Add more sauce and layer of noodles
    5. Cover with foil and bake for 45 minutes in a pre-heated 450 degree oven
